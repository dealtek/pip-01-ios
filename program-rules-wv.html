Welcome as an applicant for West Virginia Grant Program Using Participate in Program Powered by Norton Medical

Your West Virginia Grant ID is:       . Use this ID number when creating your account.

To receive the West Virginia Invests Grant, you must receive a negative result from the drug screening.  The drug screen must be taken within 60 days prior to the start of each term. Your term start date is in the letter you received. Your drug screen must be completed between the dates on the letter you received. Drug screening results taken outside of the 60-day window will not be accepted.

You must provide a driver’s license or government issued photo identification card and your drug screen authorization form at your appointment.

*If your program or start date has changed, please contact the financial aid office at your college to update your application. Changes to the program start date will change the 60-day window to complete this requirement.

Instructions on Completing the Drug Screening Process

To complete your drug screening, you will need to access the “Participate in Program” cell phone application
powered by Norton Medical Industries or website www.participateinprogram.com/mobile

You will use this application to pay for your screening, schedule your appointment, show the collector your authorization form, and access your screening results.

  • 1. Download the cell phone application “Participate in Program”
    • For Mobile Devices. Download the “Participate in Program” App on the IOS or Google Play Store and fill out the Participate in Program Application to create an account.
    • Your agency type is:     1039      for West Virginia Invests Grant Program.
    • Website: go to www.participateinprogram.com/mobile
  • 2. Create an Account
    • Once you have accessed the site, select New Application –Sign up from the main page. You must have your West Virginia Invests Grant ID # and your Driver’s License number or Government Issued Identification number to create a new account. Use your Driver’s license or other UNIQUE number like your cellphone number as your password. For returning applicants, select Current Client Login. The application should remember your password.
  • 3. Schedule your Appointment
    • Find a drug screening collection site nearest to you by clicking: Find Drug Screening Collection Sites. Applicants can use any Labcorp site that does workplace drug testing at Labcorp.com.
    • Enter the first two numbers of your Zip code and the collection sites in West Virginia will be populated
      below
      • You should schedule an appointment or walk in to the collection as soon as you can within the 60-day window of the start date of your program. The type of screening you are required to take is an Employment Drug Test. Please select Employment Drug Test when making your appointment.
    • Call for an appointment or walk in to the collection site for your test.
  • 4. Payment
    • You need to pay for your drug test on the Participate in Program app or on the application/website: www.participateinprogram.com/mobile
    • You will need to pay for your drug screen via Credit or Debit Card by clicking: Pay for Drug Screening.
    • The student is responsible for the $34 cost of the drug screen. A credit or debit card is required to submit payment. Cash payment is not accepted.
    • You must pay for the screening in full on your “Participate in Program” account prior to your drug
      screening appointment.  Payments will not be accepted at the collection facility.

      • Retesting to challenge a result for any reason will cost an additional $175 per test.

    You must have a negative screen prior to the beginning of each term to qualify for the West Virginia
    Invests Grant Program.

  • 5. Items you must bring to your appointment.
    • Drug Authorization Form is in your cell phone app.
      • The drug authorization form can be accessed via mobile device by clicking: Mobile Authorization Form to show the collector.
      • OR you can print a copy from the website, participateinprogram.com/mobile and click: Drug Authorization Form and take the authorization form to the collection site.
    • Driver’s License/ Government Issued Photo Identification card.
      • Bring a valid driver’s license or government issued photo ID
  • 6. Once you have completed your drug screening, the results will be sent within a week to the West Virginia Invests Grant Program. You can see your own results on your application or web app.
  • 7. The West Virginia Invests Grant Program office staff will only receive a positive or negative indication of your results. No further information will be shared with our office or your school.
  • 8. Once your results are reported as Negative, you will receive additional information via email from Heartland ECSI to complete your promissory note and disclosures.
  • 9. If your results are not negative, you will be contacted by the Participate in Program staff.
  • 10. For questions concerning the drug screening, you should contact Norton Medical Industries 800-243-7669 9:00AM to 7:30PM Eastern Standard Time.
